Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

Overview

Cirklon Instrument Editor ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The App does not:

  • Create user accounts
  • Track users
  • Collect analytics
  • Display advertising
  • Share data with third parties

Documents and Files

The App allows you to create, open, edit, and save instrument definition files on your Mac.

All files remain under your control and are stored only in locations that you choose. The App accesses files solely for the purpose of reading and writing the documents you explicitly select.

Network Access

The App does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

If future versions include optional online features, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features become available.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
